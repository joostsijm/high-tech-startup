Business Model Canvas

Key Partners Key Activities Key Resources
Who are our key partners?
Generative pretrained language model providers, employment candidates, hiring organisations, research institutes. 		What key activities do we perform?
Support self-empasization of candidates, trough training to gain self-awareness. Use information to produce initiatives for deployment, based on job demand. Support job seekers through coaching, and tracking of oppertunities & relationships with potential leads. 		What key resources do we need?
Knowledge on different industry sector, requirements of jobs in demand. The desires, ambition and capabilities of jobseekers. A generative pretrained language model.
Value Propositions Customer Relationships Customer Segments
What value do we deliver to customers?
Dispose wasted effort in the job searching process, through automation, build a better carreer faster. 		How do we interact with customers?
Through coaching, support jobseekers on their carreer through gaining self-awareness and insight the industry. 		Who are our target customers?
(Graduate) students, career changers, adult.
